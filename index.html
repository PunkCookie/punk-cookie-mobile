What Is PunkCookie?

The PunkCookie are 10,000 uniquely generated characters. No two are exactly alike, and each one of them can be officially owned by a single person on the Ethereum blockchain. Via market you can buy, bid on, and offer punks for sale. Each PunkCookie is unique and precious. Ownership includes your PunkCookie's creative and commercial rights. The PunkCookie are 24x24 3D pixel cookie art , generated algorithmically. Most are punky-looking guys and girls, but there are a few rarer types mixed in: Apes, Zombies and even the odd Alien.
